Slavi WEB - slaviweb.hit.bg - Layout II

This fan site is dedicated to a Bulgarian showman named Slavi Trifonov. His show, "The Slavi's Show", is the most watched show in Bulgaria and Slavi is one of the most popular persons in our country. He has 14 albums and the 15th one is due to release until the end of 2002. The site offers biography, discography, photos, audio and video stuff, articles and more. At the moment the site is online and is being updated regularly. Visit the site here or if you have problems loading it, click here for a preview.
