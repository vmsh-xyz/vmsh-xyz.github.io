Before you start downloading!

    Thank you for coming to this section. Now, to download without problems, first I recommend using GetRight! If you don't have such a program click the little icon from the right to download.

    When you click one of the links to download something and your browser open an error page, GetRight doesn't catch the link. Then, right click the link -> choose 'copy shortcut' -> open GetRight -> File -> Enter New Url -> Paste -> and click OK. Now, I hope, you are ready to download. :)

   Please, E-mail me if you have problems. Thanks for understanding!
