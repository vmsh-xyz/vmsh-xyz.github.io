Marion and Marit met when they were both five years old in a small town just outside of Oslo, Norway. It wasn't long before they started singing and dancing together. A few years later they both acted in musicals like Annie, The Sound of Music, Bugsy Malone and the Wizard of Oz. After a while Marion and Marit figured out that we wanted to do something by ourselves.

To make a long story short, they got a record deal and released a children's record (Norwegian songs for kids) in 1994. The record did pretty well, (actually it got nominated for the best children's record of the year at the Norwegian Grammy awards) and M2M's got the opportunity to perform on several TV shows, festivals, and radio programs. They had a lot of fun and were convinced that this was what they wanted to do with their lives!

When they went into the studio a year later to record another children's album, Marion and Marit realized that they wanted to do something different. they wanted to take the next step, and they started practicing guitar and piano, and began writing pop songs together. They wrote lyrics in English so more people could understand our message.

One of their demos ended up at Atlantic Records in the summer of '98 and they got a worldwide deal! Since then, they've been traveling worldwide in order to complete our first recording. They've been fortunate that some of the best songwriters and producers in the world have heard their music and voices, and to great delight, have wanted to work with them. "Shades of Purple" is the result of a lot of hard work (and tons of fun!!!).
